**EverWrestle is a fun multiplayer Racing Game, where you try to be the first at the peak. 
Knock your opponents out and throw them around, but be aware that your opponents are not the only obstacles on your way to the top.**

EverWrestle was created as part of a student project at [School4Games](https://www.school4games.net/) during late 2025.

Play the game on [Itch.io](https://s4g.itch.io/everwrestle)!

## Responsibilities
I worked as a Game Engineer, primarily responsible for gameplay and player aspects, such as feature implementation, sound implementation and traps (Props Spawner, Hammer etc.).

## Highlights
- Player
- Spawner

## Built With
- **Visual Scripting** (Blueprints)
- **C++** (Programming Language)
- **Unreal Engine** (Game Engine)
- **Perforce** (Version Control)
